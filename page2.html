Cons
In cases where there is lack of space, small wild animals are easier to keep as pets.
Large animals need lots of space and big yards to live in. In Los Angeles, big yards are not very common and are very expensive.
Some wild animals cost less to house than domestic animals.
Most wild animals, which are also called exotic, are very expensive to house and maintain.
Some animals might have difficulty surviving in the wild, due to an injury or other circumstances.
Most animals are meant to live in the wild and need to be in the wild to survive.
Certain wild animals, like hedagehogs, can be useful in controlling insects and pests in the house.
Some species of animals live in large groups. Without their groups, they might not properly develop. most wild animals grow up to be too big to live in captivity.
