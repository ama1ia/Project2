Who am I?
My name is Amalia Perez. I am an animal activist that thinks that animals should not be caged up and allowed to be free in the wild.
What am I doing?
I am rebelling against having wild animals as pets.
Why am I rebelling?
I am rebelling because these animals not only pose a threat to humans, but humans also pose a threat to animals. Wild animals are meant to stay in the wild where they have enough space and freedom to do whatever they want.

There are many different animals that have been bred to make them more pet-like. However, these pets are still wild and need to be raised with survival instincts.
