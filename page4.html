The Dangers

By adopting a wild animal, you are placing the animal in an unfamiliar habitat.
After wild animals are kept as pets for long periods of time, they no longer have the instincts they need to survive in the wild by themselves.
Certain animals need a social group to grow in, so it might not develop properly if it grows up alone.
All wild animals aren't able to make it to a loving home. Some animals are subject to brutal conditions like cramped cages, insufficient food, and insufficient water causing them to become ill or to die.

Not only is it bad for the animal to be kept as a pet, it is also inhumane to force a pet that is meant to live by themselves live as a pet in captivity.
