The Dangers

Animals that grow up to be big also grow up with some of their wild instincts, which includes acting aggressively.
Most wild animals carry diseases that are fatal for human beings. These are also known as zoonotic diseases. Some of these diseases include:
  1. Salmonella (from reptiles/amphibians)
  2. Monkey Pox (from Gambian Rats)
Animals also become more unpredictable as they get older.
Without the proper training, it is dangerous for a person to take care of a wild animal.
It takes centuries for an animal to be considered domestic. A wild animal will always be wild unless it is domesticated over centuries.
